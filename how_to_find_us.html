Travelling from the North Side

  1. Travel along Amiens Street.
  2. After train bridge, turn left onto Lower Sheriff St.
  3. Follow road right onto Commons St.
  4. At first set of traffic lights turn left onto Mayor St.
  5. Continuing down Mayor St., turn right onto Alderman Way after the college.
  6. The NCI car park is on your right hand side.

Travelling from the South Side

  1. Travel over East Link Bridge and take the 1st exit off the round about onto North Wall Quay.
  2. At the junction by Jury's Inn turn right onto Commons St.
  3. At first set of traffic lights turn right onto Mayor St.
  4. Continuing down Mayor St., turn right onto Alderman Way after the college.
  5. The NCI car park is on your right hand side.

OR

  1. Follow Pearse Street onto Tara Street.
  2. Cross Butt Bridge.
  3. Take a right onto Custom House Quay.
  4. At Jury's Inn, turn left onto Commons St.
  5. At first set of traffic lights turn right onto Mayor St.
  6. Continuing down Mayor St., turn right onto Alderman Way.
  7. The NCI car park is on your right hand side.
