Library Support

The key role of the Library is to support students to use all aspects of the library resources to maximize your academic success.

Students with certain disabilities (e.g. hearing impaired, visually impaired, mobility difficulty, specific learning difficulty) may require specific additional supports to facilitate equal access to the same resources as other students.

This is determined during your Educational Needs Assessment with the Disability Officer.

Some of the supports available include:

  • Extended library loans;
  • Access to electronic books;
  • Access to electronic readers;
  • Adapted furniture;
  • and print credit.

Academic Support

An essay support / proofreading service is provided to students with dyslexia.

The purpose of this service is to assist with structure and presentation and to highlight errors in spelling and grammar.

Essay support is not a tutoring service and does not assist with any issues relating to content.

Please see Guidelines for Essay Support Service.
